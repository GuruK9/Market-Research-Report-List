**Vitamin D Market Dynamics**

**Rising Prevalence of Vitamin D Deficiency**

Vitamin D deficiency is a global health concern, affecting various populations due to factors such as limited sun exposure, dietary habits, and lifestyle choices. This widespread deficiency has led to increased demand for vitamin D supplements and fortified foods.​

**Expansion of Fortified Food and Beverage Sector**

The food and beverage industry is increasingly incorporating vitamin D into products to address nutritional gaps. This trend is particularly notable in regions with high deficiency rates, contributing to market growth.​

**Growth in Pharmaceutical Applications**

Vitamin D's role in bone health and immune function has led to its increased use in pharmaceutical formulations. The pharmaceutical segment is expected to witness substantial growth, driven by the rising incidence of osteoporosis and other bone-related disorders.​
