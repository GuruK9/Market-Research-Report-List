**Rayon Fiber Market Segmentation:**

**By Product Type**

- **Viscose Rayon:** Dominates the market due to its softness, breathability, and affordability, making it widely used in textiles and apparel.
- **Modal Rayon:** Known for its enhanced softness and durability, modal is gaining popularity in luxury textiles and intimate wear.
- **Lyocell Rayon:** Expected to witness the highest growth rate, attributed to its eco-friendly production process and applications in premium apparel and medical textiles.
- **Cuprammonium Rayon:** Although less prevalent, it is utilized in specialized applications requiring fine and sheer fabrics.

**By Application**

- **Apparel:** Accounts for the largest rayon fiber market share, driven by consumer preference for comfortable and sustainable clothing options.

- **Healthcare & Hygiene:** Growing use in medical bandages, wipes, and personal care products due to rayon's absorbent properties.
