**Technological Trends and Innovations**

**Digital Switchgear and Smart Grids**

Digitization is enabling real-time condition monitoring, automated fault detection, and remote asset management. Switchgear is evolving from a passive safety component to an active participant in grid intelligence.

**Eco-Friendly and SF6-Free Alternatives**

As environmental regulations tighten, manufacturers are developing vacuum-based and dry-air insulated alternatives. Innovations in eco-efficient gas mixtures and biodegradable insulation are on the rise.
