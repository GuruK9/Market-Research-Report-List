**Key Vitamin K2 Market Segmentation**

**MK-7 Dominates the Type Segment**

The **MK-7 (Menaquinone-7)** subtype leads the global market, driven by its **superior bioavailability** and **longer half-life** in the human body. Extracted mainly from **fermented foods like natto**, MK-7 is scientifically validated for its ability to enhance calcium absorption and support arterial health.
