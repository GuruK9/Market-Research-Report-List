**Biological Seed Treatment Market Dynamics: Demand and Challenges**

**Dominance of Microbial Treatments**

Microbial seed treatments dominate the market, accounting for approximately 66.4% of total revenue in 2024. This growth can be attributed to the increasing use of beneficial microorganisms such as bacteria, fungi, and actinomycetes, which enhance seed health, protect against pathogens, and improve nutrient uptake. Key microbial strains such as *Rhizobia*, *Bacillus*, and *Trichoderma* play a critical role in promoting plant growth, improving stress resilience, and boosting crop productivity.

The key benefits of microbial treatments include:

- **Pathogen Suppression**: Beneficial microbes inhibit the growth of harmful pathogens, reducing the need for chemical pesticides.
- **Nutrient Enhancement**: Certain microbes help in fixing nitrogen or improving phosphorus availability to plants, resulting in healthier crops.
- **Stress Resistance**: Microbial treatments improve a plant's resilience against environmental stresses such as drought or extreme temperatures.

Among the different formulations used for biological seed treatment, liquid formulations are expected to dominate by 2032.
