**Tunnel Boring Machine Market Segmentation by TBM Type**

**Soft Ground Tunnel Boring Machines**

Soft ground TBMs are designed for soils such as clay, silt, sand, and gravel. These machines excel in maintaining face stability under variable pressure environments. Earth Pressure Balance (EPB) and Slurry Shield TBMs are two main categories:

- **EPB TBMs** maintain tunnel face pressure using excavated material.
- **Slurry Shield TBMs** circulate slurry to support the face and transport spoil.

Soft ground TBMs dominate urban metro projects, given their ability to manage complex and unstable soil conditions while minimizing surface disruption.

**Hard Rock Tunnel Boring Machines**

Hard rock TBMs are optimized for excavating strong, abrasive geological formations like granite and basalt. These include:

- **Gripper TBMs**, which use hydraulic thrust against rock walls.
- **Single Shield TBMs**, suitable for fractured rock conditions.
- **Double Shield TBMs**, enabling simultaneous excavation and lining.

Hard rock machines are integral to deep tunnels, hydroelectric projects, and mountain-crossing railway corridors.
