**By Lease Type**

1.  **Gross Lease**: The landlord covers maintenance and operational
    expenses.

2.  **Net Lease**: Popular in the industrial and retail sectors, tenants
    assume **property taxes, insurance, and maintenance costs**.

3.  **Modified Gross Lease**: A hybrid model balancing cost-sharing
    between tenants and landlords.

4.  **Percentage Lease**: Common in retail leasing, where rent is based
    on a percentage of sales.

**By Property Grade**

1.  **Prime Properties**: High-end commercial properties in **major
    metropolitan areas**.

2.  **C-Grade Properties**: Affordable leasing options, highly sought
