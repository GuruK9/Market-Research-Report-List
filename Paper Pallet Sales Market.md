The paper pallet is a lightweight, recyclable, and sustainable alternative to wood and plastic pallets. Constructed primarily from corrugated or honeycomb paperboard, these pallets cater to industries requiring cost-effective and eco-conscious shipping solutions.

- **Honeycomb Paper Pallets**: Utilizes hexagonal structures to distribute load and minimize material weight.
