**Exchange Traded Fund Market Challenges and Regulatory Considerations**

**Adoption Barriers**

Despite their growth, ETFs face challenges including:

- **Investor education gaps**, particularly in emerging markets.
- **Liquidity issues** in certain niche or low-volume ETFs.
- **Overconcentration risks** in popular indices during market corrections.

**Regulatory Oversight**

Regulatory frameworks such as **MiFID II (Europe)**, **SEC rules (U.S.)**, and **SEBI guidelines (India)** aim to enhance investor transparency, improve fund disclosures, and curb potential systemic risks arising from ETF trading volumes.
