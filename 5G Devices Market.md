**Spectrum Utilization and Technology Preferences**

**Sub-6 GHz Spectrum: The Market Mainstay**

The sub-6 GHz frequency band dominates the spectrum landscape, holding over 66% market share. Its optimal balance of coverage and speed makes it suitable for urban and rural deployments, ensuring widespread accessibility. Early adoption and network readiness have also accelerated the production and sales of sub-6 GHz 5G devices.

**Millimeter Wave (mmWave) Spectrum: The High-Speed Frontier**

Though currently less widespread due to higher infrastructure demands and shorter range, mmWave spectrum offers unparalleled ultra-high-speed data transfer and low latency, ideal for densely populated urban hotspots and enterprise applications.

**Hybrid Spectrum Devices**

Devices capable of operating on both sub-6 GHz and mmWave spectrums represent a growing niche, providing flexibility and optimal performance across varied network environments.
