**Crypto ATM Market Segmentation: Deployment by Type, Application, and Region**

**By Type: One-Way vs. Two-Way ATMs**

- **One-Way Crypto ATMs**
  Primarily enable the purchase of cryptocurrencies using fiat currencies. These machines dominate early installations due to lower operational complexity.
- **Two-Way Crypto ATMs**
  Allow both buying and selling of cryptocurrencies. Increasingly preferred due to their versatility, these ATMs cater to a more mature user base and enable liquidity for traders.

**By Application: Beyond Financial Institutions**

- **Banking Sector**
  Financial institutions are integrating Crypto ATMs to offer direct digital asset access to customers. These machines support diversification and facilitate crypto portfolio expansion.
- **Retail Sector**
  Retail environments such as malls, gas stations, and convenience stores are deploying Crypto ATMs to attract tech-savvy customers and generate passive revenue.
- **Others**
  These include airports, educational institutions, and government-linked entities exploring blockchain use cases in public finance and governance.
