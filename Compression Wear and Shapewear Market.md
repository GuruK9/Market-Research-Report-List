**Product Segmentation Analysis**

**Compression Wear: Dominant Segment with Athletic Focus**

Compression wear accounts for nearly 80% of the global market. It includes:

- **Bottoms**: Leggings, shorts, and pants engineered to boost circulation and muscle performance.
- **Tops**: Compression shirts and tanks designed to stabilize upper body muscles.
- **Sleeves**: Used widely by athletes for elbow, arm, and calf support.
- **Socks and Stockings**: Common in both athletic and medical use for reducing swelling and improving blood flow.

The preference for compression wear stems from its multifaceted benefits, such as muscle support, injury prevention, enhanced performance, and faster recovery.

New-age shapewear products integrate breathable, moisture-wicking materials with sleek designs for all-day wear, making them an essential part of modern wardrobes.
