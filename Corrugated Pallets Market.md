**Corrugated Pallets Market Drivers**

**1. Sustainability Mandates**

Corrugated pallets are 100% recyclable, making them a preferred solution for eco-conscious companies. Rising governmental regulations on packaging waste and corporate ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) responsibilities have significantly spurred adoption.

**2. Cost-Efficiency in Transport**

With a weight that is up to **60% lighter** than wooden alternatives, corrugated pallets drastically reduce shipping costs and fuel consumption, particularly in air freight and cross-border transportation.

**3. Hygiene Compliance**

Industries like pharmaceuticals and food & beverages prefer corrugated pallets due to their **resistance to bacteria, moisture, and contaminants**, meeting international hygiene standards without fumigation or heat treatment.
